Aqua Pilate

Aqua Pilate is a body conditioning programme that relies on strengthening the “core postural muscles” of your back and stomach and will help realign your body. It is performed with breathing exercises hence its very effective for your lungs. The water’s resistance against your movements results in a higher workout intensity compared to land Pilate. A massaging effect is created when your muscles are surrounded by water.
Benefits:
Improved endurance and fitness
Eases stiff joints and relaxes sore muscles
Reduced pain and fatigue (tiredness)

Aqua Yoga

The benefits of yoga need no introduction. Combining the poses of yoga with the calming effect of water, Aqua Yoga makes for a great workout! With the body bearing less weight in water, one can safely achieve even those asanas (poses) that appear complex on land. Aqua Yoga helps in improving flexibility and also improves muscle strength.
