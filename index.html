WELCOME TO AQUAROBICS

About Aquarobics

Why Aqua Exercises?
  • Fastest growing exercise programs in the world today
  • Programs for people of all ages and all levels of fitness
  • Engages upper & lower extremities through optimal ranges of motions
  • Minimizes stress on joints,resulting in a unique combination of exercise & physiotherapy
  • Water provides more resistance than air so as you move through the water; you are getting an excellent work out
Benefits

  • Helps improve flexibility and balance
  • You don’t sweat even in 40 ° celsius hot weather.
  • Protect your joints, muscles and bones since your body will be supported by water
  • Water jogging burns 11 calories per minute while on land it burns 6 calories per minute
  • Perfect for older adults, those suffering from arthritis, disabilities, blood pressure, cardiac problem and those carrying extra weight.
WHAT DO YOU NEED TO BEGIN?

Well, the most obvious is a swim suit and a swim cap. If you are worried for tanning of your skin, buy a full swim suit (full hand to full legs).


CAN I JOIN IF I DON’T KNOW SWIMMING?

Yes. Swimming is not necessary. Water balancing instruments like Pool Noodle, Aqua Dumbbells, and Waist Belts will be available in the classes.
